What is Neutrify?

Neutrify grabs news articles that are published online and puts them in one place. We then give you a variety of advanced tools that allow you to customise what news you see. This should help you figure out what's going on in the world on your own terms.

Save Time

Why waste time scrolling through countless articles you have no interest in? Neutrify allows you to choose the articles that are relevant to you, and hide the ones that aren't.

Avoid Stress

Reading the news doesn't have to come with a health warning. Avoid the stuff that irritates you by hiding articles that reference the events, topics, people and places that raise your blood pressure.

Stay Informed

Who, what, where and why? Neutrify can answer these questions for you. Take a glance at the keywords and topics mentioned in the article to see if you really want to read it.

No Interference

We don't have anyone behind the scenes choosing the news you see, nor some algorthim telling you what to read. We think it should be up to you to curate your own news.

No Agenda

Neutrify doesn't promote any single world view. All we want is for you to have the greatest amount of information available, so you can make up your own mind. We stay out of it.

No Cost

To start using Neutrify, all you need to do is sign up for an account, which costs you nothing. All you need is an email address. You'll see adverts, but they won't impede your reading experience.
