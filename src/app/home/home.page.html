Take Control Of The News

Save time

Why waste time scrolling through countless articles you have no interest in? Neutrify allows you to choose the articles that are relevant to you, and hide the ones that aren't.

Avoid stress

Reading the news doesn't have to come with a health warning. Avoid the stuff that irritates you by hiding articles that reference the events, topics, people and places that raise your blood pressure.

Stay informed, not overwhelmed

Who, what, where and why? Neutrify can answer these questions for you. Taking a glance at the keywords and topics mentioned in the article often tells you enough.

No interference

We don't have anyone behind the scenes choosing the news you see, nor some algorthim telling you what to read. If you think it should be up to you to curate the news, then you're in the right place.

No agenda

Neutrify doesn't promote any single world view. All we want is for you to have the greatest amount of information avaliable, so you can make up your own mind. We stay neutral.

No cost

To start using Neutrify, all you need to do is sign up for an account, which costs you nothing. You'll occassionally see adverts, which pay the bills and go towards developing more app features.
