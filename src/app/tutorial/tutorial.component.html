Tutorial

Welcome to the tutorial. Here you will see how to use the different tools Neutrify provides you.

Don't have time now? You can always access the tutorial again from the main menu.

Neutrify is constantly being updated with new features! Check back on this tutorial to find out how to use them.

Tutorial: Switch Layout

Try out the two different layouts available to you: Grid or List.

Tutorial: Adding Filters

You can easily add filters by clicking on one of the key terms found in an article.

Tutorial: Manual Filters

You can also manually add filters by using the filter menu. You have many to choose from.

Tutorial: Searching

Search for a single key term by clicking the search icon. Then enter your search term.

Tutorial: Political Bias

To filter by political bias, cycle the button's colour to include or exclude the bias from your feed.

Tutorial: Clearing Filters

To clear your current filters, open the filter menu and click the 'Clear Filters' button.

Tutorial: Dark Theme

To easily switch to and from dark mode, open the main menu and check the 'Dark Theme' option.
