Tutorial: Adding Filters

You can easily add filters by clicking on one of the key terms found in an article.

Tutorial: Manual Filters

You can also manually add filters by using the filter menu. You have many to choose from.

Tutorial: Searching

You can search for a single key term by clicking the search icon and then entering your search term.

Tutorial: Political Bias

To filter by political bias, cycle the button's colour to include or exclude the bias from you feed.

Tutorial: Clearing Filters

To clear your current filters, open the filter menu and click the 'clear filters' button.

Tutorial: Dark Theme

To easily switch to and from dark mode, open the main menu and check the 'Dark Theme' option.
